# Sales Performance Dashboard (2023) | Tableau



## Overview
This project presents a Sales Performance Dashboard (2023) developed in Tableau to deliver a comprehensive and actionable view of business performance through KPIs, category-level insights, and time-based analysis.



## Key Metrics (KPIs)

The dashboard highlights the following performance indicators:

- **Total Sales:** $733K (+20.4% YoY)  
- **Total Profit:** $93K (+14.2% YoY)  
- **Total Quantity:** 12K (+26.8% YoY)  

### Features:
- Monthly trend comparison  
- Identification of highest and lowest performing months  
- Visibility into seasonality and performance fluctuations  



## Subcategory Analysis

The Sales & Profit by Subcategory section provides a dual perspective on revenue and profitability.

### Insights:
- High-performing categories include Phones, Chairs, and Binders  
- Some subcategories generate strong sales but low or negative profit  
- Helps identify cost inefficiencies, pricing gaps, and low-margin products  



## Time-Series Analysis

The Sales & Profit Over Time section uses dual line charts to evaluate trends.

### Features:
- Average reference lines:
  - Sales ≈ $14K  
  - Profit ≈ $2K  
- Classification of performance as above or below average  

### Insights:
- Sales show consistent growth with periodic spikes  
- Profit demonstrates volatility and occasional losses  



## Year-over-Year Comparison

- Covers performance comparison from 2020 to 2023  
- Highlights overall growth trends across key metrics  



## Business Value

This dashboard enables:
- Quick performance monitoring  
- Identification of loss-making segments  
- Data-driven decision-making  
- Better understanding of trends and risks  



## Conclusion

By combining high-level KPIs with detailed analytical insights, this dashboard serves as an effective tool for understanding business performance, improving profitability, and supporting strategic decision-making.
